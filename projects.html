Gress Custom Craft Corner Website

This is a website designed for my mother's craft business. I designed it using Showit, a website building service. I started off using a template, and then edited it for the final website. I ended up having to make the shop pages essentially from scratch since the template did not provide adequate pages for it. My mother provided most of the text and images, while I did all of the web design. For the shop, I encorporated Shopify, a service for online stores. I then used the Shopify buy button extension in order to allow people to purchase items through the website. The biggest challenge was allowing people to have custom orders. This is currently the last part of the website that needs to be finished. Neither Shopify or Showit provide adequate options for this. The solution I came up was to have a form pop up when a button pressed, and then having the add to cart button appear once the form is submitted. This is not ideal for me, since this means that the form will still be submitted even if the item is ultimately not purchased, but it is the best solution I could come up with.
